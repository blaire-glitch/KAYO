## ðŸš€ Getting Started

### What is KAYO?
KAYO (Kama Afya Ya Orodha - Youth Assembly) is the official registration system for the ACK Diocese of Nambale Youth Assembly. As a Parish Chair, you are responsible for:

- Registering delegates from your parish
- Making payments for your delegates
- Managing delegate information
- Downloading/printing delegate tickets and badges

### System Requirements
- A smartphone, tablet, or computer with internet access
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- A valid email address

## ðŸ” Registration & Login

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the Registration Page**
   - Go to the KAYO website
   - Click **"Register"** or **"Sign Up"**

2. **Fill in Your Personal Information**
   - **Full Name**: Enter your complete name as it appears on official documents
   - **Email**: Use a valid email you can access (required for login verification)
   - **Phone Number**: Your mobile number (optional but recommended)

3. **Select Your Church Details**
   - **Local Church**: The name of your specific local church
   - **Archdeaconry**: Select your archdeaconry from the dropdown
   - **Parish**: After selecting archdeaconry, choose your parish

4. **Create a Password**
   - Choose a strong password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Confirm your password by typing it again

5. **Submit for Approval**
   - Click **"Create Account"**
   - Your registration will be sent to admin for approval
   - âš ï¸ **Note**: Only ONE chair per parish can be approved

6. **Wait for Approval**
   - You will receive notification when your account is approved
   - Only approved accounts can log in

### Logging In

1. Go to the KAYO website
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password**
3. Click **"Sign In"**
4. **OTP Verification** (One-Time Password):
   - A 6-digit code will be sent to your email
   - Enter the code within 10 minutes
   - Click **"Verify"**

> ðŸ’¡ **Tip**: If you don't receive the OTP, check your spam folder or click "Resend Code"

## ðŸ‘¥ Registering Delegates

### Single Delegate Registration

1. **Access Registration**
   - From Dashboard: Click **"Register Delegate"** button
   - From Sidebar: Click **"Register"**

2. **Fill in Delegate Information**

   **Personal Details:**
   - **Full Name** (Required): Delegate's complete name
   - **Gender** (Required): Male or Female
   - **Phone Number**: Delegate's contact (optional)
   - **Email**: Delegate's email (optional)

   **Church Details:**
   - **Archdeaconry**: Auto-filled from your profile
   - **Parish**: Auto-filled from your profile
   - **Local Church**: Delegate's specific local church

   **Category** (Optional):
   - **Delegate**: Regular attendee (default)
   - **Leader**: Youth leaders
   - **Speaker**: Event speakers
   - **VIP**: Special guests

   **Accommodation:**
   - Select if delegate needs accommodation

   **Special Needs:**
   - Note any dietary requirements or accessibility needs

3. **Submit Registration**
   - Click **"Register Delegate"**
   - A unique ticket number will be generated
   - The delegate will appear in your list

### Bulk Registration (Multiple Delegates)

1. Click **"Bulk Upload"** from the Delegates page
2. Download the Excel template
3. Fill in delegate information in the spreadsheet
4. Upload the completed file
5. Review and confirm the imported delegates

## ðŸ’³ Making Payments

### Payment Methods

KAYO supports the following payment methods:

1. **M-PESA** (Recommended)
   - Pay directly from your phone
   - Instant confirmation

2. **Cash Payment**
   - Pay to designated officials
   - Requires admin confirmation

3. **Bank Transfer**
   - Transfer to official account
   - Requires admin confirmation

### How to Pay with M-PESA

1. **Access Payment Page**
   - Click **"Pay"** in the sidebar, OR
   - Click **"Pay Now"** from the payment alert

2. **Select Delegates**
   - Check the boxes next to unpaid delegates you want to pay for
   - Or click **"Select All Unpaid"**

3. **Review Amount**
   - System calculates total: Number of delegates Ã— KSh 1,000

4. **Enter M-PESA Number**
   - Enter the phone number to receive the STK push
   - Format: 0712345678 or 254712345678

5. **Initiate Payment**
   - Click **"Pay with M-PESA"**
   - You'll receive an M-PESA prompt on your phone

6. **Complete Payment**
   - Enter your M-PESA PIN on your phone
   - Wait for confirmation

7. **Confirmation**
   - Payment status updates automatically
   - Delegates marked as "Paid"
   - Receipt available in payment history

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Account Issues

**Q: I can't log in. What should I do?**
> A: Check if your account has been approved. New registrations require admin approval. If approved and still having issues, use "Forgot Password" or contact support.

**Q: I didn't receive the OTP code.**
> A: Check your spam/junk folder. Wait 2 minutes and click "Resend Code". Ensure your email address is correct.

**Q: Can I change my parish?**
> A: No. Each chair is assigned to one parish. Contact admin if you need to change.

### Registration Issues

**Q: I accidentally registered a delegate twice.**
> A: If unpaid, you can delete the duplicate. If paid, contact admin for assistance.

**Q: Can I register delegates from other parishes?**
> A: No. You can only register delegates from your assigned parish.

**Q: What if I make a mistake in delegate details?**
> A: If the delegate is unpaid, you can edit the details. If paid, contact admin.

### Payment Issues

**Q: My M-PESA payment failed but money was deducted.**
> A: Wait 24 hours for automatic reversal. If not reversed, contact support with your M-PESA message.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
> A: Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Contact admin with your reason.

**Q: Can I pay for delegates individually?**
> A: Yes! Select only the delegates you want to pay for.

### General Questions

**Q: What is the registration fee per delegate?**
> A: KSh 1,000 per delegate.

**Q: Is there a deadline for registration?**
> A: Check the current event details for registration deadlines.

**Q: Can delegates register themselves?**
> A: No. All delegates must be registered by their Parish Chair.
